### Notes for review

AndroDR is a security tool that scans for malware and stalkerware. It displays security-related terms (malware, spyware, stalkerware, C2 server) in its scan results. These are technical security terms, not violent or harmful content. The app does not depict or encourage harmful behavior — it detects and helps remediate it.
